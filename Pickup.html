Click & Collect

Shop online and pick up your order at NP Boutique — quick, convenient, and safe.

How It Works

  1. Add your favorite items to the cart and select "Click & Collect" at checkout.
  2. Wait for our confirmation email or SMS notifying you that your order is ready for pickup.
  3. Visit our boutique during pickup hours with your order confirmation and valid ID.

Order Hold Period

Please collect your order within 7 days of notification. Orders not collected within this timeframe may be canceled or restocked.

Payment Options

All Click & Collect orders must be paid online at the time of purchase.

Returns & Exchanges

You can return or exchange items picked up in store by following our standard returns policy. For details, visit our Returns page or contact customer support.
